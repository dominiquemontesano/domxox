Website #1: Denim: A Data Deep Dive

To visit website!

Design: 9/10

The design for this website is very interactive for tying together what seems to be a russian brand. The designer chose somber background colors withbright colors circled into sections. This site draws you in.

Usability: 8/10

Besides it being a language I can't understand (lol) It looks quite user-friendly. The home page takes a good 30 seconds to bring you to the content and kind of brings you in the future with this effect of movement and blurring.

The more you click-click, the more content is made available.

Creativity: 8/10

This website is a great example of interactive media. It is far from boring.

Content: 10/10

The website is a collection of cinematic photographs for a clothing brand.

Final Note

All in all, i believe this website is pretty neat
